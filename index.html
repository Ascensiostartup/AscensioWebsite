About Ascensio

Ascensio is a student-founded initiative created to empower engineering students by providing them with real-world internships and project support.

What We Do

We offer hands-on internships, guide innovative projects, and connect students with practical industry-level opportunities. Our programs cover fields like AI/ML, Web Development, IoT, CAD Design, Data Analytics, and more.

Our Mission and Motive

Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and industrial expertise. We strive to nurture a generation of engineers who are industry-ready, confident, and innovative thinkers prepared to tackle real-world problems.
